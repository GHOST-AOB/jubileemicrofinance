Establishment & Mission

Governance-Led
Financial Excellence.

Since 2012, Jubilee Microfinance has been at the forefront of Northern Uganda's economic transformation, delivering capital with precision and integrity.

A Legacy of Trust

Founded in Arua City, Jubilee Microfinance began with a simple mission: to provide the local "Omwoli" (entrepreneur) with the tools they need to scale. Over a decade later, we have grown into a multi-regional institution serving thousands of families.

We are fully licensed by the Uganda Microfinance Regulatory Authority (UMRA), ensuring that our operations meet the highest standards of consumer protection and financial stability.

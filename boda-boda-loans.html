Boda Boda
Ownership Path.

Stop renting, start owning. We provide comprehensive financing for Bajaj Boxer and TVS motorcycles.

Drive Your Way to Prosperity

The Boda Boda sector is a vital part of Uganda's transport infrastructure. Jubilee Microfinance empowers youths and entrepreneurs to own their tools of trade through low-friction asset financing.

Ownership Roadmap

Step 1: Deposit

Pay a small initial deposit (as low as UGX 500,000) to get started.

Step 2: Training

Safety and financial literacy training at our regional hubs.

Step 3: Operation

Ride and earn while paying daily or weekly installments.

Step 4: Title

Ownership of the Log Book is transferred to you upon completion.

Built-in Protection

All our motorcycle loans include comprehensive insurance and GPS tracking for your safety and peace of mind.

Requirements

  • Valid Riding Permit (Class A).
  • Two reliable guarantors (one relative).
  • Proof of residence (LC1 Letter).
  • Commitment to daily repayments via Mobile Money.

Saving groups
Stronger together — group financial solutions

We support VSLA and SACCO-style saving groups with group loans, financial training, and record-keeping tools. Register your group and unlock better rates.

WG
Women's saving group
5–30 members · Minimum UGX 500,000 saved
Groups receive access to a shared loan facility of up to 3× total savings. Ideal for women entrepreneurs and market traders.
Preferred rates available
AG
Agri saving group
5–50 members · Farming communities
Designed for farming cooperatives. Groups access seasonal loans for inputs, irrigation, and post-harvest storage with flexible repayment windows.
Harvest-cycle repayment
YG
Youth enterprise group
5–20 members · Ages 18–35
Youth groups get startup financing, free business mentorship, and digital financial literacy sessions to launch and grow small ventures.
Mentorship included

Community SACCO
50+ members · Registered body
Formally registered SACCOs benefit from higher loan ceilings, savings mobilization support, and access to our financial reporting tools.
Enhanced loan limits
